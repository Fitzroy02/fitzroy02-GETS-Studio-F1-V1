## ğŸ“– Executive Summary

### What is the Emotional Tone Device?

The **Emotional Tone Device** is a regulated clinical service providing **emotional tone analysis** with **watch-based heart monitor integration** for early intervention in patient care and practitioner wellbeing.

**Key Features:**
- Real-time emotional state monitoring
- Heart rate correlation and irregular rhythm detection
- Early intervention alerts (stress, anxiety, cardiac events)
- NHS-compliant security (DSPT, Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 27001)
- 10% ecological allocation (trees + ocean conservation)

**Clinical Applications:**
- Patient mental health monitoring (inpatient/outpatient)
- Practitioner burnout prevention
- Post-surgical emotional support
- Cardiac rehabilitation programs
- End-of-life care comfort monitoring
- A&E/ICU high-stress environment monitoring
