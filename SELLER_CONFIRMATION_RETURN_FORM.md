## How This Form Works

**Step 1: Buyer Requests Refund**
- Buyer completes `RETURN_REFUND_FORM.md` within 7-day viewing period
- Platform reviews and approves/denies request

**Step 2: Buyer Returns Artwork**
- Buyer ships artwork back to artist using tracked delivery
- Buyer provides tracking number to platform

**Step 3: Artist Receives & Inspects**
- Artist receives returned artwork
- Artist completes **this form** (Seller's Confirmation)
- Artist inspects condition and completes condition report

**Step 4: Artist Submits Confirmation**
- Artist emails completed form to artists@getsstudio.com
- Includes photographic evidence (if condition issue)

**Step 5: Platform Processes Refund (Within 48 Hours)**
- Platform verifies return confirmation
- Platform processes refund to buyer
- Platform adjusts artist's account (recoups original payout)
- Ecological allocation remains permanent (not recouped)

**Step 6: Dispute Process (If Necessary)**
- If artwork damaged beyond acceptability, artist initiates dispute
- Platform mediates between artist and buyer
- Independent assessment if needed
- Resolution: Full refund, partial refund, no refund, or insurance claim
