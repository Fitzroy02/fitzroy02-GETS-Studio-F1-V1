This Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) establishes a formal framework for collaboration between **Living Hopenism** (Party A) and **[Partner Organization]** (Party B).

## 2. Shared Commitments & Principles

### 2.1 Core Values

Both parties commit to upholding the following principles in all collaborative activities:

**Transparency:**
- All workflows, contracts, and financial flows are documented and auditable
- Revenue distribution (70/20/10 split) publicly documented
- Quarterly reports issued to stakeholders
- Blockchain verification of all allocations and certificates

**Contributor Dignity:**
- Fair pricing with no hidden fees or deductions
- Contributors control their metadata, names, bios, links
- Participatory annotation (community-driven, not algorithmic)
- Contributors retain copyright; platform holds distribution license only

**Ecological Anchors:**
- 10% of all revenues allocated to ecological projects (60% trees / 40% oceans)
- Quarterly Eco Impact Certificates issued
- Blockchain-verified allocations
- Alignment with NHS Net Zero targets (2040)

**Ethical Technology:**
- Clinical services meet NHS-level security (DSPT Level 2, Cyber Essentials Plus, ISO 27001)
- GDPR compliance (UK/EU standards)
- Clinical Safety Case Report (DCB 0129) for medical devices
- Evidence-based claims supported by peer-reviewed research or pilot data
- No vendor lock-in; open standards (HL7 FHIR APIs)

**Global Stewardship:**
- Every upload, license, or transmission treated as civic act
- Global distribution networks (15 countries represented)
- Public Impact Reports (quarterly, for media/funders/public)
- Centre network vision (UK ‚Üí EU ‚Üí North America ‚Üí Asia-Pacific)
