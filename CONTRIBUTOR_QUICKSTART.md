## 10. â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I upload the same content to other platforms?
**A:** No. GETS Studio requires exclusive content. Uploading material from Spotify, YouTube, SoundCloud, or other platforms violates our content policy and will result in removal.

### Q: Is explicit music allowed?
**A:** Yes. Explicit music is fully allowed and receives the same payout structure, ecological impact, and treatment as all other content. Just mark it as explicit during upload.

### Q: How long before I get paid?
**A:** Payouts are processed monthly, typically around the 1st of each month for the previous month's streams.

### Q: What if my content gets zero streams?
**A:** No streams = no payout for that month. However, your content remains available and can earn in future months. Consider promoting, improving metadata/tags, or creating content aligned with civic/impact themes for priority placement.

### Q: Can I see who likes my content?
**A:** Aggregate like counts are visible. Individual user data is not shared to protect privacy.

### Q: How are community tokens different from cash?
**A:** Tokens are redeemable for platform services (hosting, equipment, airtime) but cannot be converted directly to cash. They're designed to support community stations with resources rather than cash payouts.

### Q: Do I control the ads on my content?
**A:** Ads only appear on Movie Home Channel (free tier). They are internally controlled and civic-aligned â€” no commercial radio ads. Music/podcast subscriptions and Movie Channel premium are ad-free.

### Q: Can I delete my content after uploading?
**A:** Yes, via your contributor dashboard. Note that past streams/likes will still count toward your lifetime ecological impact, but you won't earn new streams after deletion.

### Q: What happens to the ecological anchors?
**A:** 10% of all revenue is automatically allocated to tree planting and ocean conservation projects. You'll see transparent reporting on where funds go, including partner organizations and impact metrics.

### Q: Can I collaborate with other contributors?
**A:** Yes! Add collaborators in the metadata during upload. Payouts are split according to percentages you specify. Collaborative content often performs better due to combined audiences.
