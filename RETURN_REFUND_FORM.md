## How This Form Works

**Step 1: Buyer Submits Form**
- Complete all sections (1-7)
- Attach required photographic evidence
- Email to: buyers@getsstudio.com with subject "Refund Request ‚Äî [Artwork Reference Number]"

**Step 2: Platform Reviews (Within 48 Hours)**
- Customer support team evaluates request
- Reviews original listing description, images, condition report
- Contacts artist for response/evidence/defense
- Makes decision: Approved, Denied, or Under Investigation

**Step 3: Return Process (If Approved)**
- Buyer returns artwork to artist using tracked/insured shipping
- Buyer retains tracking number
- Buyer notifies platform when item has been returned (email with tracking number or artist confirmation)

**Step 4: Refund Processing (Within 48 Hours After Return + Notification)**
- Platform verifies item returned to artist
- Platform processes refund to buyer's original payment method
- Buyer receives refund confirmation email with transaction ID
- Artist's account adjusted (original payout recouped)

**Step 5: Appeal Process (If Denied)**
- Buyer may appeal decision within 7 days of denial
- Appeal reviewed by senior management
- If still denied: Dispute resolution process (mediation, arbitration, litigation per Section 8 of Terms & Conditions)
