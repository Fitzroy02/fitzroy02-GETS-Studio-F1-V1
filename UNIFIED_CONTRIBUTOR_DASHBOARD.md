### Adapting Template for Different Report Types

#### Daily Report
- **Date Range**: December 7, 2025 (single day)
- **Streams**: Smaller numbers (500-1,000)
- **Earnings**: Daily share of monthly pool
- **Trees**: Partial tree counts (0.05-0.5 trees)
- **Focus**: Quick snapshot, milestone tracking

#### Weekly Report
- **Date Range**: December 1-7, 2025 (7 days)
- **Streams**: Medium numbers (3,000-5,000)
- **Earnings**: Weekly accumulation
- **Trees**: 1-3 trees typically
- **Focus**: Trend analysis, week-over-week comparison

#### Monthly Report
- **Date Range**: December 1-31, 2025 (full month)
- **Streams**: Large numbers (15,000-20,000)
- **Earnings**: Full payout amount
- **Trees**: 5-15 trees typically
- **Focus**: Payment confirmation, tax documentation

#### Quarterly Report
- **Date Range**: October 1 - December 31, 2025 (3 months)
- **Streams**: Very large numbers (60,000-80,000)
- **Earnings**: Cumulative across 3 months
- **Trees**: 30-50 trees typically
- **Focus**: Strategic overview, annual planning
