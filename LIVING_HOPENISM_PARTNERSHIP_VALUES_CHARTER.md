## Charter Governance

### Review & Amendment

**Annual Review:**
- This Charter will be reviewed annually on or near the anniversary of signing
- Both parties will assess partnership alignment with Charter principles
- Any gaps, concerns, or opportunities for improvement will be documented
- Amendments to Charter require mutual written agreement

**Amendment Process:**
1. Either party proposes amendment in writing with rationale
2. Other party reviews and responds within 30 days
3. If agreed, amendment is drafted and signed by both parties
4. Amended Charter replaces previous version, with version history maintained

### Dispute Resolution

**If Charter Principles Are Violated:**
1. **Step 1 - Direct Communication:** Raise concern directly with partner representative
2. **Step 2 - Formal Notice:** If unresolved, issue formal written notice citing specific Charter provision
3. **Step 3 - Escalation:** Escalate to senior leadership at both organizations
4. **Step 4 - Mediation:** Engage neutral third-party mediator if needed
5. **Step 5 - Termination:** If unresolvable, either party may terminate partnership with 90 days' notice

**Good Faith Commitment:**
Both parties commit to good faith efforts to resolve disputes in alignment with Charter principles before resorting to termination.
